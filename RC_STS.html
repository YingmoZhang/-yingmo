Synopsis

This is also a Directed Study Program, distinguished by its resemblance to a reading group, conducted under the supervision of Prof. Paul Apisa. The reading material covers concepts from algebraic topology, including homology, cohomology, relative homology, and the strata of Moduli Spaces; it also incorporates ideas from algebraic geometry, such as algebraic varieties and manifolds, as well as dynamics in geometry, including Teichmüller space and Teichmüller flow. Our primary reference was a survey authored by Alex Wright,which focuses on topics such as translation surfaces and their dynamics. Over the course of approximately two months, we meticulously studied the first four chapters of this survey and engaged in several exercises provided by Alex Wright to reinforce and expand upon the material. This endeavor ultimately facilitated my first Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU), details of which can be found in here.
